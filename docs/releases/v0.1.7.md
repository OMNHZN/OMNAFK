## v0.1.7

This release cleans up the app layout so the everyday controls are easier to reach while deeper options stay available when you need them.

### Improved

- General is now grouped around setup, timing, action, and safety.
- Advanced timing, recovery, monitor placement, maintenance, and about details are collapsed by default.
- Settings separates startup, detection, hotkeys, notifications, app mode, interface, updates, and maintenance.
- About keeps update and support actions visible while moving license and diagnostics details into a cleaner section.
